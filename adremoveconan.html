Who Are We?

English

Hello! Our team currently consists of four people. We use Zoom to discuss current problems we are working on such as Informatics competition challenges and other related stuff. We generally use python to solve these problems, mostly consisting of reading a file, extracting data and manipulating the data to write into an output file as an answer to a problem. Our members consist of Conan L, Ayana F, Oliva S and Kevin L.

At first, we were only a WeChat group that came into existence after the Australian Informatics Competition. It was when Ayana Fridkin struck an idea. She used HTML, CSS ad JavaScript to create a chatting website dedicated for the team to chat on anytime.
