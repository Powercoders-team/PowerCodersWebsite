HTML for Beginners | Module Two: Adding detail to your website

Your website is looking good! Let's add some images.

To add an image, look it up on the web (I've used a bonfire but choose anything you like).

Next, right-click your image and choose Open image in new tab.

Save your image to my_website and give it a name with a .jpg tag (I've named mine bonfire.jpg).

Next, open up index.html and put in an <img src='(name of image file)'>.

index.html
<img src='bonfire.jpg'>
<h1>My Website Title</h1>
<p>My content</p>

My Website Title

My content

This will put your image into your website!
